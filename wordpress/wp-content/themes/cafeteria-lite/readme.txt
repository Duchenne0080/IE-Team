== Description ==
Cafeteria Lite is a clean, robust, modern, minimalist and powerful theme for food and restaurant website. This food WordPress theme helps to create a professional and elegant websites of food related business. It is capable of handling the needs of food providers, suppliers and creates online presence of your website in unique style. This multi-functional theme is best suited for cafe, restaurants, pubs, fast foods, coffee shops, catering businesses and any other related businesses. Even if it is adaptable for different types of business, a food blog or recipes magazine looks like fit perfect with this theme.
